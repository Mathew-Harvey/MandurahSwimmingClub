## Mandurah Swimming Club - Our Coaches

### Head Coach: Hyojin "Sharky" Son

Appointed Head Coach of Mandurah Swimming Club.

Sharky has an impressive International coaching record, having previously spent 6 years as Head Coach of Manjimup Marlins Swimming Club. Previous to this she has coached at various clubs in South Korea and Canada.

Sharky's primary focus will be on developing swimmers mentally and physically as a person whilst maximising their talent and potential with a focus on maintaining a strong team culture, mental edge and training with the right technique.

Sharky is excited to lead Mandurah Swimming Club to be a dominant force in swimming and will be a great mentor for all our coaches.

### Junior Coaches

Appointed Junior Coaches of Mandurah Swimming Club: Chelsea Maley, Jemma Thomson, Charlotte Vreeken, Aiden Francesconi, Kaiden Buss-Dawson and Sofia Novoselskaia.

Mandurah Swimming club has a strong ethos on developing our members, not only to reach their swimming potential but other aspirations such as coaching.

The club commenced a 'Development Training and Coaching Program' in 2022 to identify and develop swimmers that may want to do work as coaches.

All of our junior coaches are accomplished swimmers in their own right and now have the opportunity to pass on their extensive knowledge to our up and coming junior swimmers.
