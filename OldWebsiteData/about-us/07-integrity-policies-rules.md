## Mandurah Swimming Club - Keeping everyone safe

Our swimming club policies ensure that all swimmers are safe and provide guidelines for both parents and swimmers to uphold the integrity of the sport of swimming.

Mandurah Swimming Club fully incorporates Swimming Australia's Safe Sport Framework (https://www.swimming.org.au/integrity-policies-rules/safe-sport-framework).
