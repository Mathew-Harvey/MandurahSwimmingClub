## Club merchandise and uniforms are available to order and purchase through the club.

The official colours of the club are Blue, Teal and white. When representing the club at swim meets, we ask for club uniforms including swimming cap are worn. Swim caps are available to purchase from the Club Office for $12.00. Our online uniform shop is only open every few months for a period of time.

**Online Uniform Store:** https://www.meccasports.com.au/mandurah-swimming-club/ (by Mecca Sports)
