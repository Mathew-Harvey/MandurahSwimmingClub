## 2024-2025

The 2024-25 Bio Island Australian Junior Excellence (JX) program will recognise performances from 1 May 2025 to 30 April 2025.

Within the JX program, Swimming Australia recognise swimmers aged 9-13 years with a gold, silver, bronze or blue standard, based on their best swimming performance/s corelating to the times set by Swimming Australia.

To qualify for the program, swimmers need to be a member of an Australian swimming club and achieve a qualifying time at an approved meet.

Visit Swimming Australia for qualifying times for the 2024-2025 season: https://www.swimming.org.au/sites/default/files/assets/documents/JX%202021-22%20Qualifying%20Criteria%20and%20Standards_0.pdf
