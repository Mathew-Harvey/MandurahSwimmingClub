## Purple | Blue Squad
**Level:** Junior Introductory
**Age Range:** 7+

### Purple Squad
- Minimum Criteria: Swimmers must be able to demonstrate Freestyle, Backstroke and Breaststroke over a minimum distance of 50 meters. Swimmers are required to attend 2-3 training sessions per week. All Swimmers must have a SWA Membership.
- Preferred Sessions: 2-3 per week
- Session Duration: 60-90 mins
- Equipment: Swim Cap, Goggles, Fins, Pull Buoy, Kickboard, Drink Bottle

### Blue Squad
- Minimum Criteria: Swimmers must be able to demonstrate Freestyle, Backstroke and Breaststroke over a minimum distance of 50 meters. Swimmers are required to attend 2-3 training sessions per week. All Swimmers must have a SWA Membership.
- Preferred Sessions: 3-4 per week
- Session Duration: 120 mins
- Equipment: Swim Cap, Goggles, Fins, Kickboard, Drink Bottle, Pull Buoy, Front Snorkel, Hand Paddles

## Green Squad
**Level:** Junior Intermediate
**Age Range:** Not specified

### Minimum Criteria
- Swimmers should be able to cover the following distances continuously: 200m Individual Medley, 200m Freestyle, 200 Backstroke, 200m Breaststroke, and 100m Butterfly.
- Swimmers are required to attend between 3 to 4 training sessions per week.

## Yellow Squad
**Level:** Junior Intermediate
**Age Range:** 9+

### Minimum Criteria
- Swimmers who are aiming to compete at the State level and are training specifically for competitive purposes, should also have independent proficiency in using the pace clock during training.

**Age Range:** 10+

## Red Squad
**Level:** Advanced
**Age Range:** 12+

### Minimum Criteria
- Swimmers who have achieved state qualifying times or are working toward them, are approaching finals or top positions at the state level, and can consistently perform repetitive endurance and strength training.

## Black Squad
**Level:** Advanced
**Age Range:** 13+

### Minimum Criteria
- Swimmers who have qualified for Nationals or are working toward them, and are actively preparing to compete at the Australian Championships. Additionally, swimmers should consistently achieve top positions at State competitions.

## Fitness Squad
**Level:** Fitness
**Age Range:** 13+

### Minimum Criteria
- Swimmers aged 13 years and older who can demonstrate proficiency in all strokes over a distance, and who wish to train for fitness and health. These swimmers do not compete in meets.
- Swimmers are invited to attend Club Nights throughout the seasons, Club Championships (Mar/Apr) and the Mandurah LC Challenge (Nov).
- All Swimmers must have a SWA Membership.

### Details
- Preferred Sessions: 3-4 per week
- Session Duration: 60-90 mins
- Equipment: Swim Cap, Goggles, Fins, Kickboard, Drink Bottle, Pull Buoy, Front Snorkel, Hand Paddles
