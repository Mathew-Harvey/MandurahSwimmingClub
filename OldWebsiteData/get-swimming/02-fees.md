## Swimming WA Membership Fees

Members of Mandurah Swimming Club are required to register with Swimming WA which provides comprehensive insurance cover and eligibility to compete in a variety of different events. The Swimming WA fee is paid online via My Swim Results and is due 1st of October each year.

| Category | Description | Fee |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| Annual (Competitive) | Full membership for Swimmers 9+ years | $153.55 |
| Participation | Swimmers 9+ years who swim for fitness and those who have just entered the competitive swimming scene | $92.66 |
| Junior Dolphins | Swimmers aged 5-8 years of age transitioning from learn-to-swim | $46.90 |
| Parent/Guardian | Swimming WA requires each family with a swimmer under 18 to have a parent or guardian register | $5.62 |
| Coach | For all coaches working toward ASCTA accreditation and all current ASCTA accredited coaches | $33.28 |
